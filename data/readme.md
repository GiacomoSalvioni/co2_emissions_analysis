Datasets

### 1. **annual-co2-emissions-per-country.csv**
**Source:** Annual CO₂ emissions by country - Global Carbon Budget (2025) – with major processing by Our World in Data. “Annual CO₂ emissions” [dataset]. Global Carbon Project, “Global Carbon Budget v15” [original data]. Source: Global Carbon Budget (2025) – with major processing by Our World In Data 
**Coverage:** 1750-2024  
**Variables:**
- `Entity` - Country/region name
- `Code` - ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 country code
- `Year` - Year of observation
- `Annual CO₂ emissions` - Total CO₂ emissions (tonnes)

**URL:** https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/annual-co2-emissions-per-country



### 2. **co-emissions-per-capita.csv**
**Source:** CO₂ emissions per capita - Global Carbon Budget (2025); Population based on various sources (2024) – with major processing by Our World in Data. “CO₂ emissions per capita” [dataset]. Global Carbon Project, “Global Carbon Budget v15”; Various sources, “Population” [original data]. Source: Global Carbon Budget (2025), Population based on various sources (2024) – with major processing by Our World In Data
**Coverage:** 1750-2024  
**Variables:**
- `Entity` - Country/region name
- `Code` - ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 country code
- `Year` - Year of observation
- `Annual CO₂ emissions (per capita)` - Emissions per person (tonnes)

**URL:** https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/co-emissions-per-capita

### 3. **co2-by-source.csv**
**Source:** CO₂ emissions by fuel or industry type - Global Carbon Budget (2025) – with major processing by Our World in Data. “Other industry” [dataset]. Global Carbon Project, “Global Carbon Budget v15” [original data]. Source: Global Carbon Budget (2025) – with major processing by Our World In Data
**Coverage:** 1750-2024  
**Variables:**
- `Entity` - Country/region name
- `Code` - ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 country code
- `Year` - Year of observation
- `Coal` - CO₂ from coal (tonnes)
- `Oil` - CO₂ from oil (tonnes)
- `Gas` - CO₂ from gas (tonnes)
- `Cement` - CO₂ from cement production (tonnes)
- `Flaring` - CO₂ from gas flaring (tonnes)
- `Other` - Other industrial sources (tonnes)

**URL:** https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/annual-co2-other-industry

### 4. **population.csv**
**Source:** Population (historical + projections to 2100) - UN, World Population Prospects (2024) – processed by Our World in Data. “Population, medium projection – UN WPP” [dataset]. United Nations, “World Population Prospects” [original data]. Source: UN, World Population Prospects (2024) – processed by Our World In Data
**Coverage:** 1950-2100 (projections from 2024)  
**Variables:**
- `Entity` - Country/region name
- `Code` - ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 country code
- `Year` - Year of observation/projection
- `all years` - Total population
  
**URL:** https://ourworldindata.org/explorers/population-and-demography?tab=table&indicator=Population&Sex=Both+sexes&Age=Total&Projection+scenario=Medium&country=CHN~IND~USA~IDN~PAK~NGA~BRA~JPN


### 5. **gdp-per-capita-worldbank.csv**
**Source:** GDP per capita - Eurostat, OECD, IMF, and World Bank (2025) – with minor processing by Our World in Data. “GDP per capita – World Bank – In constant international-$” [dataset]. Eurostat, OECD, IMF, and World Bank, “World Development Indicators 122” [original data]. Source: Eurostat, OECD, IMF, and World Bank (2025) – with minor processing by Our World In Data
**Coverage:** 1990-2024  
**Variables:**
- `Entity` - Country/region name
- `Code` - ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 country code
- `Year` - Year of observation
- `GDP per capita` - GDP per capita, PPP (2017 $)
- `Region` - World Bank geographic region

**URL:** https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/gdp-per-capita-worldbank
